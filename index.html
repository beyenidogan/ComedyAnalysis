Stand-up comedy has become an increasingly popular form of comedy in the recent years. Live comedy shows generate hundreds of millions of revenues but the comedians can also reach audiences beyond the halls they are performing through streaming services, podcasts and social media. Watching stand-up comedy can be entertaining and relaxing but can also invite you to the minds of performers who share their most intimate thoughts or explore untapped social issues and provoke conversation. This project provides an visual exploration of the works of stand-up comedians who performed in their recent years, looking at a closer look at what and how they talk about.


This site is best viewed on desktop browser.
